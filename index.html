Hi, I am Anju Paul.

My journey to a Data Scientist from an Oracle Database Administrator was not smooth. I had to set myself in the active-learning mode again after years of leaving the University.

Once started , It was so fascinating that I quit my 8+ years full-time job at Infosys and joined MSc Applied Data Science at Anglia Ruskin University, Cambridge, United Kingdom. I learned Python programming, Statistics, Research Methodology, Data Mining and Machine Learning. I am super proud that I completed the course with DISTINCTION, exactly 10 years after my previous graduation, competing with the young and fresh brains.

I learned more and more from free online courses, from various data scientists, and by doing projects. I have done an Internship at Data Glacier, where I have worked on different modules of company projects, proof of concepts using Python, Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Feature Engineering, Model Governance, Data Visualization, Model Deployment, and stakeholder communication.

It took a lot of passion, dedication, and constant striving toward the goal when having a career change or restarting after a career break.

I also write various articles on different topics in data science, they are quite simple and easy to understand for anyone. Do check out that as well.
